# Home
Welcome! In this tutorial, you will patch your copy of LittleBigPlanet 3 to connect to the LBPOnline custom server, instead of the long dead stock servers.

## What You Need:
- A PS4 or PS5 console
- A copy of LittleBigPlanet 3 (PS4, physical or digital)
- A USB flash drive with at least 1 GB of free space recommended
- An internet connection, and a Discord account

## What You Should Know
::: danger
This server will put you at a very slim risk of a **ban from PlayStation Network.** **Use at your own risk.**
:::
